Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug in a game?

You can report bugs by using our contact form and selecting the "Report a Bug" option in the subject field. Please provide details about the game, the bug you encountered, and steps to reproduce it.

How can I partner with AnimeGamesHub?

We're always open to partnerships with game developers, anime content creators, and gaming platforms. Please email us at partnerships@animegameshub.com with your proposal or use our contact form selecting "Partnership Opportunity".

Are all games on the platform free?

Yes, all games on AnimeGamesHub are free to play. Some games may offer optional in-game purchases or premium features, but the core gameplay is always free for our users.
